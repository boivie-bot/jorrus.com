Engagement models

I
Clarity Sprint
A focused, bounded engagement for leaders who are stuck. We diagnose the real problem (which is often not the stated problem), synthesize competing inputs, and deliver a clear, prioritized path forward. Fixed fee. Typically 2–4 weeks.
Ideal for: complex decisions, strategic deadlock, new ventures
II
Ongoing Advisory Retainer
A monthly relationship for executives, founders, and principals who need a senior thinking partner in their corner. No templates, no junior handoffs. Direct access to Jorrus principals across strategy, growth, capital, and sensitive decisions.
Ideal for: CEOs, family office principals, founding teams
III
Capital Advisory
Full-cycle support for founders and executives raising capital — pitch readiness, market positioning, financial architecture, investor identification, and transaction support from first call to close. Deep expertise in Aerospace, Deep Tech, and adjacent sectors.
Ideal for: seed through growth-stage raises, M&A, restructuring
IV
Transformation Projects
Defined-scope engagements for organizations navigating major change — new strategy, new structure, new market, or new leadership. We work alongside your team, not above it, and we leave frameworks you can actually use.
Ideal for: pivots, post-merger integration, market entry
